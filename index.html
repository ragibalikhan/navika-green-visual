The $2 Trillion Maritime Industry Has a Blind Spot

Much attention is given to cleaning up what comes out of a ship's funnel‚Äîbut this ignores a massive source of emissions: the carbon created long before a vessel ever touches the water.

The construction of a single ship requires thousands of tonnes of steel and immense energy, creating a significant embedded carbon debt that the industry has largely overlooked.

Navika addresses the entire vessel lifecycle‚Äîfrom construction to operation‚Äîrevolutionizing how ships are made, powered, and maintained.
